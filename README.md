# AI-Based-Disease-Detection-Through-Symptoms
AI Based Disease Detection Through Symptoms
1. Introduction
The rapid growth of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) has brought revolutionary
changes to the healthcare domain. One of the major challenges in healthcare is the early identification of
diseases, as many illnesses share common symptoms. This often leads to confusion, delayed diagnosis, and
increased dependency on medical professionals for preliminary assessments.
The project “AI Based Disease Detection Through Symptoms” addresses this challenge by developing an
intelligent, web-based disease prediction system. The system predicts possible diseases based on symptoms
entered by the user and provides additional health-related recommendations such as disease descriptions,
precautions, medications, diet plans, and workout suggestions. This system is designed to support
preliminary healthcare awareness and decision-making.
2. Objectives
The main objectives of the project are:
• To accurately predict diseases based on user-provided symptoms using AI and ML techniques.
• To reduce the time and effort required for preliminary disease identification.
• To provide users with clear disease descriptions for better understanding.
• To suggest precautions that help minimize health risks.
• To recommend medications, diet plans, and workout routines for overall well-being.
• To deploy the system as a user-friendly web-based application.
